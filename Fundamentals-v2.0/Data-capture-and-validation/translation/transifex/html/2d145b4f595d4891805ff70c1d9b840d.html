Data Entry app: Offline Data Entry

Unstable internet access doesn’t mean that data entry needs to be done on paper and then copied later into your Health Management Information System (HMIS). With DHIS2, you can input your data even when you lose your internet connection and then upload the data to your DHIS2 server when you have internet access again. In this video, you’ll learn how simple it is to enter data offline and then upload your data to your DHIS2 server using the web-based Data Entry app. Click the play button below to start.
