Overview

In this exercise, you will install the DHIS2 Android Capture app and then log in to the app.

Instructions

Image 1 - The DHIS2 Capture app in the Google Play Store

Install the DHIS2 Android Capture app:
  1. On your Android device, open the Google Play Store
  2. Search for DHIS2 Capture
  3. Select Install

Image 2 - The DHIS2 Capture app login screen

Logging in to the DHIS2 Android Capture app:

Please choose a username and password from the following table based on the first letter of your last name.

  1. Once the DHIS2 Capture is installed, open the application
  2. Enter the Trainingland (Entry): URL https://academy.aggregate.dhis2.org/entry
  3. Enter your username
  4. Enter your password
  5. Click the Log in button
  6. After successfully logging in, you will see a user experience notification message indicating the application will send error logs associated with your user id in order to improve its performance. You can click on the privacy policy to learn more. Once you have read the notification message, click ‘Continue’
  7. Your credentials will be authenticated then the DHIS2 configuration and data will sync. Once the data have synced, you will be taken to the home page of the DHIS2 Capture app
